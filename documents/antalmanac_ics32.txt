COURSE DESCRIPTION
Programming with Software Libraries. Construction of programs for problems and computing environments more varied than in I&C SCI 31. Using library modules for applications such as graphics, sound, GUI, database, Web, and network programming. Language features beyond those in I&C SCI 31 are introduced as needed. 4 units. Lower Division. Satisfies GE II, GE Va, and GE Vb.

ENROLLMENT AND GRADES
Total enrolled historically: 10,435 students. Average grade: B- (2.9 GPA). 276 students enrolled as P/NP.

PREREQUISITES
Requires: I&C SCI 31 with a minimum grade of C. Is a prerequisite for I&C SCI 33 among other courses.
