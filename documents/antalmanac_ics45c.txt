COURSE DESCRIPTION
Programming in C/C++ as a Second Language. An introduction to the lexical, syntactic, semantic, and pragmatic characteristics of the C/C++ languages for experienced programmers. Emphasis on object-oriented programming, using standard libraries, and programming with manual garbage collection. 4 units. Lower Division.

ENROLLMENT AND GRADES
Total enrolled historically: 9,411 students. Average grade: B (3.1 GPA). 112 students enrolled as P/NP.

PREREQUISITES
Requires: I&C SCI 33 with a minimum grade of C, or EECS 40 with a minimum grade of C. Is a prerequisite for I&C SCI 46 among other courses.
