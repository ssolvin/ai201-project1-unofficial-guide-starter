COURSE DESCRIPTION
Introduction to Programming. Introduction to fundamental concepts and techniques for writing software in a high-level programming language. Covers the syntax and semantics of data types, expressions, exceptions, control structures, input/output, methods, classes, and pragmatics of programming. 4 units. Lower Division. Satisfies GE II and GE Vb.
